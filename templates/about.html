Hi, I'm Kaleb

I am an amateur photographer living in Sacramento, California. I grew up in northwestern Maryland.

I became interested in photography by shooting film on a Minolta X-7 as a teenager. I have now transitioned to primarily digital photography. I am particularly interested in black and white and impressionistic photography.

I shoot with an Sony alpha 7III with a variety of modern and vintage lenses.

  • Sony Sonnar T* FE 55mm f/1.8
  • Tamron 28-200mm f/2.8 - f/5.6
  • Helios 44-2 50mm f/2
  • Pentax Takumar SMC 135mm f/2.5
