Welcome

me in Hokkaido, Japan 2023

I am a photographer and engineer living in Sacramento, California. I grew up in northwestern Maryland and have slowly been migrating west as an adult. I am currently a doctoral student studying how we can advance the design of morphing autonomous aircraft by studying the flight dynamics and control of birds.

I became interested in photography by shooting film on a Minolta X-7 as a teenager, but have transitioned to primarily digital photography. I am particularly interested in black and white and impressionistic photography.
