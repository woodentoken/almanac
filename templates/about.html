Welcome

I am a photographer and engineer living in Sacramento, California. I grew up in northwestern Maryland and have slowly been migrating west as an adult. I am currently a doctoral student in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of California, Davis.

I became interested in photography by shooting film on a Minolta X-7 as a teenager, but have transitioned to digital photography as an adult. I am particularly interested in black and white and impressionistic photography.

I am available for portraits, wedding, and event photography. Please reach out to discuss your needs.

I shoot with an Sony alpha 7III with a variety of modern and vintage lenses.

  • Sony Sonnar T* FE 55mm f/1.8
  • Tamron 28-200mm f/2.8 - f/5.6
  • Helios 44-2 50mm f/2
  • Pentax Takumar SMC 135mm f/2.5

Additionally, I have a variety of filters and use a GODOX V860II-S flash to control environmental lighting.
